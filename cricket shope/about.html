About SHAHFAHAD Shope

Since 2018

Crafting Championship Legacies

SHAHFAHAD Shope was born out of a deep-seated obsession with the game's finest details. In cricket, the margin between a boundary and an edge is fractions of a millimeter. That's why we set out to source and distribute equipment that offers absolute precision, elite power transfer, and maximum safety.

What started as a boutique workshop selecting elite willow for local pros has evolved into a global supplier. We work hand-in-hand with legendary bat-makers and materials scientists to supply equipment that feels like an extension of the player.
